# Legal, Ethics, and Responsibility

> **Critical notice:** KOVIL MAP is a professional tool for security auditing, education, and research. The maintainers are not responsible for damage or legal consequences caused by misuse. You are solely responsible for your actions.

## Dual-Use

KOVIL MAP is a dual-use tool. Whether a use is lawful depends on authorization and intent.

- legitimate use: authorized pentests, research, and home labs
- illegal use: attacking third-party networks, collecting data without consent, or disrupting services

## Legal Context

Laws vary by country. Use the project only within the law that applies to you.

### Network Access and Cracking

- attempting to crack a network you do not own or are not authorized to test can be illegal
- active attacks such as deauthentication or unauthorized interception can escalate the impact

### Privacy

- MAC addresses, SSIDs, and location data can be personal or sensitive
- do not publish or reuse data from third parties without a legal basis

## Professional Use

If you use KOVIL MAP professionally:

1. get written authorization
2. define the scope
3. define the time window
4. define the allowed techniques
5. keep evidence of authorization

## Wardriving Ethics

- avoid publishing precise home-network locations
- do not collect more data than you need
- treat captured data as sensitive

## User Code of Conduct

1. Only test networks you own or are authorized to audit
2. Do not disclose sensitive third-party data
3. Avoid actions that can disrupt services
4. Assume full responsibility for your use of the tool

> When in doubt, test only in your own controlled lab.
